About ChattO

The inspiration behind ChattO came from observing the challenges that small restaurants face in managing customer interactions efficiently. Our team wanted to create a solution that would help streamline operations, enhance customer experience, and provide valuable insights through automation and intelligent chat solutions. This project was developed as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School. Check out Holberton School for more details.
